Oliver Kamm operated Oliver Kamm/5BE gallery from 2002 to 2008. The gallery received multiple reviews in publications such as The New York Times, Artforum, Flash Art, Art News, Art in America, The Village Voice, Time Out New York and many other publications.

In June of 2008 the gallery closed to focus on secondary market sales, consulting and continuing to represent a small group of artists.
